## 🧠 Overview

Lernivo is an AI-powered German learning platform designed specifically for international students moving to Germany for higher studies.

Unlike generic language apps, Lernivo focuses on **real-life outcomes**:

* surviving daily life
* clearing visa interactions
* getting part-time (Werkstudent) jobs
* preparing for full-time interviews

The platform combines structured learning with AI-driven speaking practice to help users go from **zero to job-ready German**.

---

## 🔥 Problem

International students face major challenges:

* traditional apps don’t teach real-life German
* lack of speaking practice for interviews
* difficulty in pronunciation and confidence
* poor preparation for visa and job scenarios

This leads to:

* missed job opportunities
* social isolation
* reduced confidence

---

## 💡 Solution

Lernivo solves this by:

* providing structured CEFR-based learning paths
* enabling real-time AI conversations
* offering pronunciation feedback
* simulating job and visa interview scenarios
